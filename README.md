## 😳 About me
I am dedicated to fostering inclusivity and community, building decentralized governance models, and enabling collaborative decision-making in complex and emergent environments.

I am Source (see [Work with Source](https://workwithsource.com/)) for [ODIN](https://www.odin.eco), a network of innovators who believe that decentralized innovation networks will replace centralized corporations as the primary way we solve hard problems in the world. ODIN experiments with and mentors evolutionary organizational principles, and creates ways to scale through connecting high-trust spaces so that innovation networks and their members can thrive.

I am a co-founder of [Andamio](https://www.andamio.io). Andamio is building a Trust Protocol on Cardano — verifiable credentials, on-chain trust, and tools for communities and organizations to build networks of trust that are secure and transparent. V2 is now live on mainnet; my focus has shifted from launch to stewarding operational excellence and the admin/finance backbone as we grow enterprise adoption.

I am also the Chairman of the Board for the [Zenwest Buddhist Society](https://zenwest.ca), a Rinzai Zen center in Victoria, BC.

These days I think of myself as a **"cybernetic farmer-monk"** — Zen practice, the land, and AI engineering woven into a single life. I'm drawn to the intersection of blockchain, AI, decentralization, bio-region/local re-vitalization, and Integral theory, and increasingly to the work of *tending*: attention, body, land, and community. I am a practicing Zen Buddhist.

## 👀 What I am obsessed about
- **The human's role in an AI world** — I'm experimenting heavily with what it means to be human when AI can do more and more of what we used to do. Agentic workflows, and the question of where human agency actually matters. This is what keeps me up at night.
- **Agent-native workflows** — building AI agent systems that use governance patterns (Sociocracy 3.0, Source Principles) instead of hierarchical orchestration. The metaphor: Shardplate from Brandon Sanderson's Stormlight Archive — agents forming power armor around a human, not swarms acting autonomously.
- **Compound / agentic engineering** — closing the loop. I call my version **ODARE** (Observe → Decide → Act → Record → Evaluate), and I'm mapping a "five levels of AI engineering maturity" spiral.
- **Claude Code** — Anthropic's CLI tool for AI-assisted development. I use it as my daily operating system — morning rituals, task management, Obsidian vault integration, meeting transcription, code reviews, and more.
- **e-ink readers** — using my [Boox Palma 2](https://shop.boox.com/products/palma2) as a distraction-free device (Kindle + Obsidian only, no social media).
- **Decentralized & off-grid communications** — my newest love. Resilient, self-sovereign messaging that doesn't depend on cell towers, ISPs, or the internet at all: [MeshCore](https://meshcore.io/) and LoRa mesh (I'm running a repeater), Mark Qvist's cryptography-first [Reticulum](https://reticulum.network/) stack, and long-range [Wi-Fi HaLow](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/IEEE_802.11ah) (802.11ah). The same self-sovereignty thread as my governance work — but for the physical comms layer.
- **Warhammer 40,000** — deep in the lore right now via the *Horus Heresy* novels, plus painting and tabletop. Always up to connect with others into it (Victoria-local or online).
