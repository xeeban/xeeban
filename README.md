ODIN experiments with and mentors evolutionary organizational principles, and creates ways to scale through connecting high-trust spaces so that innovation networks and their members can thrive.

I am a co-founder of [Andamio](https://www.andamio.io). Andamio is building a Trust Protocol on Cardano — verifiable credentials, on-chain trust, and tools for communities and organizations to build networks of trust that are secure and transparent. We're launching V2 with a focus on enterprise adoption and operational excellence.

I am also the Chairman of the Board for the [Zenwest Buddhist Society](https://zenwest.ca), a Rinzai Zen center in Victoria, BC.

In my free time, I enjoy diving deep into the intersection of blockchain, AI, decentralization, bio-region/local re-vitalization, and Integral theory. I am also a practicing Zen Buddhist.
